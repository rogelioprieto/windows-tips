## Problem:
I have a Microsoft account on Windows 10 but I don't need it.

## Solution:
Convert into a local account.

## Steps:
1. Open ```Settings```.
2. Click on ```Accounts```.
3. Click on ```Your info```.

Click the ```Sign in with a local account instead``` option.
